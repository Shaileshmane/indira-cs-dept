About Department

Welcome to U.G. Department of Computer Science, Indira Gandhi (Sr.) College, CIDCO, Nanded. Department of Computer Science was running Since 2001.

Computer Science is one of the most important subjects & Education of Computer Science is important to todayâ€™s modern, digital society. Computer Science that offers a great opportunities & variety of options in every field of life in India and abroad. Career in computer science is considered one of the most high-paying jobs in private and public sectors, government Departments, Commercial organizations Business organizations, and the Manufacturing sector & also in Universities and Research sectors.

The aim of the Department is to prepare students Leadership in the Profession as well as Higher Education and to prepare in some Specialized area of computer science for jobs in Industries, Business or Government, requiring Computing skills. And to enhance their skills.

The Department is having well equipped laboratory with all infrastructural facilities and internet facility. The Department is highly equipped with all necessary equipment required for U.G. The Computer Science Lab is supported by LCD projector which is used by the staff in process of teaching like PPT presentations, Programming practical, videos and computer aided techniques related to syllabus. The teaching learning process is facilitated by conducting seminars, Webinars for students, conducting unit tests, conducting extra classes, doubt solving sessions. Guest lectures by expertise and excursion tour are also arranged.

The strength of the department is experienced and qualified faculty. The faculty members are highly qualified strong academic background. The faculty is actively engaged in teaching and other extra-curricular activities for students. The faculty members have published several research articles in reputed national and international journals and books. The alumnus of this Department occupies position of good responsibilities in different industries like TDS, TRACES, Tech Mahindra etc.
